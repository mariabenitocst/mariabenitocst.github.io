About me

Welcome to my webpage! I am a PhD student under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Iocco at the Instituto de Física Teórica in São Paulo: a research institute for theoretical physics at Universidade Estadual Paulista. I obtained my Master's degree in elementary particles and cosmology at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid. So far, my work has been focused on dynamical studies of our Galaxy as a probe of the dark matter distribution in the Milky Way and a way of constraining modified gravity theories. I am interested in galaxy formation & evolution models and the very nature of the dark matter particle.
